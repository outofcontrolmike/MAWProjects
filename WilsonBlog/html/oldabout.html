My path and story

Hello there! You can call me Mike or Michael. I'm not a very experienced programmer or Web Developer, however I am working my way towards learning the necessary skills to become either one. I recently graudated with a Computer Information Science degree from a growing community college named Ozark's Technical Community College, December of 2019. I started my journey in Web Development in the Spring of 2018. I obtained rounded knowledge about what goes into building websites and programming. I also learned about how to interact with a client and how to configure their needs for a project.

Education alone has not been enough for me to feel content with the career path. I realized the struggles I endured were a good thing for me since it helped me grow. It prepared me for feeling overwhelmed and lost with how much there is to learn in Web Development and Programming.

I'm hoping before the year 2023 I'll be able to make dynamic websites and systems for real world problems.

Thanks for reading about me!
