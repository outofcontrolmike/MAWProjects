My Journey So Far

Hello there! You can call me Mike or Michael. I'm not a very experienced programmer or Web Developer, however I am working my way towards learning the necessary skills to become either one. I recently graudated with a Computer Information Science degree from a growing community college named Ozark's Technical Community College, December of 2019. I started my journey in Web Development in the Spring of 2018. I obtained rounded knowledge about what goes into building websites and programming. I'm hoping before the year 2023 I'll be able to make dynamic websites and systems for real world problems.
